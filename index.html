FAQ

Common questions

Still unsure about something? Reach out and we'll walk you through it.

What areas do you service?

Prince George, BC and the surrounding area, residential and commercial. Not sure you're in range? Send your address when you request service.

How does online booking work?

Pick an open slot on our single crew calendar and pay a time-slot fee plus parts deposit to confirm. Reconciled on your final invoice.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — fully licensed and insured, residential and commercial.

Can I reschedule or cancel a booking?

Yes — just reach out and we'll move your slot. Deposits carry over to the new visit. Cancelling within 48 hours of appointment may require a rescheduling fee.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes, subject to crew availability — same-day bookings (within 24 hours) carry a $200 rush surcharge on top of the standard time slot fee.

What if my job needs more than the estimated time?

We'll flag it before continuing. Your prepaid fee and deposit are credit toward the final invoice, not a fixed quote.
